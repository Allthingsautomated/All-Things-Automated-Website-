## Step 2: Make It Legal (Copyright Strategy)

**The problem:** Raw NHL game footage is copyrighted. The NHL aggressively polices it.

**The solution:** Make your content *transformative* so it qualifies as fair use. Here's how:

- **Add commentary** — text overlays like "This save was INSANE — watch the glove" or voiceover reactions
- **Create compilations with a narrative** — "Top 5 saves of the week" with your rankings and opinions
- **Use trending TikTok audio** — puts the clip in a new creative context
- **React to the footage** — even a simple text-based reaction ("no way he just did that") transforms the content
- **Mix multiple sources** — combining clips from different games into a themed compilation adds originality

**What NOT to do:**
- Don't repost raw, unedited NHL clips
- Don't use official broadcast audio without adding something to it
- Don't claim the footage is your own original content
