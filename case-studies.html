Full Home Automation

Lakewood Ranch Estate - Complete Control4 Integration

The Challenge: A 5,500 sq ft new construction home needed a unified system to control lighting, climate, security, audio, and motorized shades -- all from a single interface.

The Solution: We designed and installed a full Control4 system with 42 Lutron lighting zones, 8 Luma security cameras, multi-zone Sonos audio, automated Lutron shades, and a Nest climate system -- all accessible from wall-mounted touchscreens, the homeowner's phone, and voice commands.

Smart Lighting

Palmer Ranch Remodel - Lutron RA3 Lighting Upgrade

The Challenge: An existing 3,200 sq ft home wanted to modernize their lighting without running new wires or major renovation. They wanted scene control, dimming, and automated schedules.

The Solution: We replaced all switches and dimmers with Lutron RA3 wireless devices across 18 zones, programmed custom scenes for entertaining, relaxing, and nighttime, and integrated with their existing Alexa ecosystem for voice control.
