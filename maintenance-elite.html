Elite Care

White-glove service for premium smart homes. Quarterly inspections, same-day support, and proactive system monitoring.

What's Included in Elite Care

Elite Care is our premium maintenance plan for clients with sophisticated Control4 or Lutron RA3 whole-home systems who expect exceptional service and maximum uptime. With four professional visits per year, you receive hands-on expert oversight every three months.

Each quarterly on-site inspection involves a top-to-bottom review of your entire system. Our technicians perform comprehensive diagnostics on all devices, test every automation scenario you use, verify security system functionality, check system performance metrics, and make proactive adjustments to optimize your experience. You also get detailed documentation of the system's condition.

Elite Care members receive same-day priority support during business hours. Call and get answers the same day your issue arises. Your problems go directly to the front of our queue. You also receive two free service calls per year (valued at $198), plus 20% discount on all additional work and new installations. When you're ready to expand your system, you jump the installation queue and get priority scheduling.

Beyond reactive troubleshooting, we proactively monitor your system where possible to catch issues before they affect you. We handle all firmware and software updates, maintain comprehensive documentation, and serve as your personal smart home concierge.

Plan Details

What's Included

Quarterly Inspections

Four professional on-site visits per year (approximately every 3 months). Each visit includes comprehensive system diagnostics, testing of all automation scenarios, security verification, performance analysis, and detailed documentation. Our technician provides recommendations and handles any needed adjustments on the spot.

Same-Day Support

Call us during business hours and get a response the same day. Your issues take priority and are handled with urgency. When you have a problem with your Control4 or Lutron system, you get direct access to our most experienced technicians without waiting in queue.

2 Free Service Calls

Two complimentary diagnostic and repair visits per year (a $198 value). Use them for troubleshooting, system adjustments, or minor fixes. Beyond your two free visits, all additional service calls receive a 20% discount off standard rates.

Proactive Monitoring

We don't just wait for problems to happen. We proactively monitor your system where possible to identify and address issues before they impact you. Combined with quarterly on-site inspections, this ensures maximum uptime and performance.

Priority Scheduling

When you want to expand your system or add new devices, you jump to the front of our installation queue. Get your new smart home features installed quickly without long wait times. Plus, 20% discount on all new installation work and equipment.

20% Discount On Everything

Beyond your two free service calls, you receive a 20% discount on all additional service calls, new device installations, system upgrades, and any other work. This discount applies to equipment, labor, and custom programming.
