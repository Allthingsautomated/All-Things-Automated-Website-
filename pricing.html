Common Questions

Pricing FAQ

Everything you need to know about our pricing and packages.

Are the prices all-inclusive, or are there additional costs?

Our packages are starting prices that typically include design, installation, and initial setup. The final cost depends on your home's complexity, the number of rooms, device selections, and customizations. We provide a detailed quote during the initial consultation with zero surprises or hidden fees.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Absolutely. Many clients start with Starter or Home Essentials and add features over time. Our systems are built to grow with your needs. We can seamlessly integrate new devices and expand functionality whenever you're ready.

What if my home is smaller or larger than a typical installation?

We customize every solution. Smaller homes might find our Starter package perfect, while larger estates often invest in Full Automation or custom configurations. During your initial consultation, we'll assess your space and recommend the best fit.

What's included in the ongoing support?

Support covers troubleshooting, system adjustments, software updates, and technical assistance. Starter includes 30 days, Home Essentials includes 90 days, and Full Automation includes one year of priority support with direct access to our team.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes. We partner with flexible financing providers to make premium automation accessible. Ask about payment plan options during your consultation.

What brands and systems do you work with?

We partner with industry-leading brands including Lutron, Control4, Nest, Honeywell, Ring, and Luma. We select the best technology for your specific needs and budget, ensuring quality and reliability.
