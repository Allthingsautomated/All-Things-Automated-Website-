Common Questions

Pricing FAQ

Everything you need to know about our pricing and packages.

Are the prices all-inclusive, or are there additional costs?

Our packages are starting prices that include design, installation, and initial setup. The final cost depends on your home's complexity, the number of rooms, device selections, and customizations. We provide a detailed quote during the initial consultation with zero surprises or hidden fees.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Absolutely. Many clients start with Smart Start or Home Essentials and add features over time. Our systems are built to grow with your needs. We can seamlessly integrate new devices and expand functionality whenever you are ready.

Do you work with builders and interior designers?

Yes. We offer standalone smart home blueprint and lighting design services specifically for custom builders and interior designers. We work directly with your team during pre-construction and rough-in phases, and design fees are credited toward any installation we perform.

What is included in a service call?

A service call is $99 and includes a full system assessment, troubleshooting, and a binding quote on any repairs or adjustments needed. If you are on one of our maintenance plans, you may have free service calls included.

Do you handle commercial projects?

Yes. We serve offices, retail spaces, restaurants, medical facilities, hotels, HOA common areas, and property management companies. Commercial work is billed at $110/hr and every project receives a custom quote based on scope and complexity.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes. We partner with flexible financing providers to make premium automation accessible. 0% APR is available on qualifying purchases, with terms from 6 to 60 months. Ask about payment plan options during your consultation.

What brands and systems do you work with?

We partner with industry-leading brands including Lutron (Caseta, RA2, RadioRA 3), Control4, Nest, Ecobee, Honeywell, Ring, and Luma Surveillance. We select the best technology for your specific needs and budget.
