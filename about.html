About All Things Automated

We're a Sarasota-based smart home company dedicated to bringing the latest technology into homes with expertise, care, and professionalism.

Built On A Simple Belief

We started All Things Automated with one core conviction: technology should simplify your life, not complicate it. Too many smart home companies sell expensive systems and disappear. We're different. We stay involved, support our clients long-term, and take pride in every single installation.

Our team brings decades of combined experience in automation, electrical work, and customer service. We're licensed, insured, and hold ourselves to the highest standards. Whether you're automating a single room or your entire home, we treat your project with the same meticulous attention.

Our Core Values

Expert Installation

Every system we install is handled by experienced technicians who understand the technology inside and out. No shortcuts, no guessing.

Premium Brands Only

White-Glove Service

Serving the Gulf Coast
