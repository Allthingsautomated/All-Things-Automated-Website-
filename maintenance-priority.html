Priority Care

Our most popular plan. Fast support, regular maintenance, and real peace of mind for your smart home.

What's Included in Priority Care

Priority Care is the ideal choice for homeowners with multi-room smart home setups or security systems who want professional maintenance without worrying about extended wait times. With two service visits per year, you get regular professional oversight of your entire smart home system.

Our technicians perform bi-annual on-site health checks that inspect, test, and fine-tune your entire system. They verify firmware is up to date, ensure all devices are communicating properly, test connectivity across all zones, inspect sensor batteries, and confirm all security and camera systems are operating at peak performance.

Priority Care also includes priority phone and email support with a 4-hour response time during business hours. Need help immediately? Call us and get an answer within 4 hours instead of waiting days for email responses. You also receive one free service call per year with a $99 value, plus 15% discount on any additional service visits beyond that.

Many common issues can be diagnosed and resolved remotely through our cloud connectivity. This means faster fixes without waiting for a technician to schedule and travel to your home. Combined with regular updates and monitoring, Priority Care keeps your smart home in top shape.

Plan Details

What's Included

Bi-Annual Inspections

Two professional on-site visits per year (approximately every 6 months). Each visit includes thorough system inspection, device testing, connectivity verification, and fine-tuning to ensure optimal performance. Your technician provides recommendations for improvements or expansions.

Priority Phone Support

Call our support team during business hours and get a response within 4 hours. No waiting days for email replies. Your issues are prioritized, and our technicians tackle them immediately. Perfect for when something isn't working as expected and you need quick help.

1 Free Service Call

Each year includes one complimentary diagnostic or repair visit worth $99. Use it for troubleshooting an issue, making adjustments, or installing a new device. After your free call is used, additional visits receive a 15% discount off the standard rate.

Remote Troubleshooting

Many common issues like connectivity problems, device pairing, or setting adjustments can be resolved remotely. Our technicians log into your system via secure cloud connection, diagnose the issue, and fix it without needing to schedule an on-site visit. Fast, convenient, and cost-effective.

Software Updates

We handle all firmware and software updates remotely to ensure your system stays secure and runs smoothly. New features, security patches, and performance improvements are deployed automatically, keeping your smart home protected and current.

Who This Plan Is For

Priority Care is designed for homeowners with multi-room smart home setups or growing security systems. If you have smart lighting throughout your home, a comprehensive security system, smart locks, climate control, and cameras, this plan delivers the professional support and maintenance you need.

Ideal for you if:

  • You have smart devices in multiple rooms throughout your home
  • You want professional inspections twice per year
  • You need faster support response times with priority phone access
  • You want to use your free annual service call for maintenance or upgrades
  • You value proactive maintenance and don't want to be caught off guard by system issues
