Basic Care

Peace of mind for your smart home. Essential maintenance and support starting at just $29 a month.

What's Included in Basic Care

Basic Care is designed for homeowners with simple smart home setups who want to keep their systems running smoothly without breaking the bank. Once a year, a certified technician will visit your home to conduct a comprehensive health check of all your smart devices and systems.

During the annual on-site visit, we inspect every connected device, test wireless connectivity to ensure strong signal strength, verify that all firmware is current and secure, check battery levels on wireless sensors and smart locks, and confirm that all camera feeds are actively recording. This proactive approach catches potential issues before they become problems.

Between visits, we handle software and firmware updates remotely, ensuring your devices always have the latest security patches and feature improvements. When you have questions or run into issues, you can reach our support team via email with a guaranteed 48-hour response time during business days.

If you ever need a service visit beyond your annual checkup, Basic Care members receive a 10% discount on standard service calls, reducing the typical $99 fee to $89.

Plan Details

What's Included

Annual Health Check

Once per year, a certified technician performs a comprehensive on-site inspection of your entire smart home system. We test connectivity, verify firmware versions, inspect sensor batteries, and confirm all cameras and security devices are functioning correctly.

Remote Updates

We push software and firmware updates remotely to keep your devices secure, stable, and running the latest features. This eliminates the need for you to manually manage updates and ensures your system is always protected with the newest security patches.

Email Support

Submit questions or issues via email and receive a response within 2 business days. Our support team provides guidance on system adjustments, troubleshooting, and device configuration to keep everything running smoothly.

Service Discounts

If you need a service call beyond your annual checkup, you save 10% on all additional visits. Instead of paying $99 per service call, you pay just $89, making it even more affordable to address any issues that arise.

Who This Plan Is For

Basic Care is perfect for homeowners with simple to moderate smart home setups. If you have smart lighting in one or two rooms, a smart thermostat, or a few connected devices in your home, this plan provides the peace of mind and support you need.

Ideal for you if:

  • You want annual professional system checks without paying for premium service
  • You prefer email communication and don't need immediate phone support
  • You have 1-2 rooms of smart devices or a few individual smart devices
  • You want to save money on future service calls
  • You value simplicity and predictable, affordable support
